# AUMetric Platform
## Executive Summary

---

## The Opportunity

The wealth management industry is ripe for disruption. Financial advisors face a perfect storm of challenges:

- **Outdated Technology**: Legacy marketing platforms like FMG Suite ($200M+ ARR) are decades old and poorly designed
- **Compliance Burden**: Every piece of marketing content must comply with FINRA and SEC marketing rules
- **Fragmented Tools**: Advisors juggle multiple disconnected platforms for social media, email, ads, and compliance
- **AI Demand**: Firms are under pressure from leadership to adopt AI but lack compliant solutions

**AUMetric solves all of these problems in a single, AI-powered platform built specifically for wealth management.**

---

## What We're Building

AUMetric is an AI-powered marketing platform that enables financial advisors to:

1. **Generate Compliant Content Instantly** - AI creates social posts, emails, and ads that automatically incorporate FINRA and SEC marketing rules
2. **Publish Across Channels** - Post directly to LinkedIn, Facebook, and email from one dashboard
3. **Automate Outreach** - LinkedIn messaging sequences and email nurture campaigns run on autopilot
4. **Ensure Compliance** - Built-in approval workflows prevent any content from going live without compliance sign-off
